Audition

Do you have what it takes to be the champion of The Voice of Walla Walla? On February 3, 2018, find out as twelve singers from WWU, Whitman, and WWCC compete for a grand prize of $100!

Auditions for The Voice are on January 22, so prepare your song, fill out the application, and come to audition at the Havstad Alumni Center on Monday, January 22 between 7 and 9 PM. If you can't make it, you can also audition at Walla Walla Community College on January 23rd from 5 to 7 PM and at Whitman College on January 25th from 5 to 7 PM.

Please note that you need to bring your backing track, background singers, accompanists, or band to the auditions. ASWWU will have a drum, but bring your snare and cymbals. For more questions, please email aswwu.social@wallawalla.edu.
