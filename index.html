About Shane and Shane

The Associated Students of Walla Walla University are proud to present Shane and Shane in concert, May 13 at 6PM in the Winter Educational Complex on Walla Walla University’s Campus. Visit shaneandshane.com to find their music and begin listening!

100% of ticket sales will be donated to ASWWU's Global Service project “On This Rock", an initiative that is building a much-need trade school in Haiti. Visit our website to learn more about this project. Do not miss this opportunity to worship God and make a difference in the lives of the people of Haiti!
